Export Functionality:
The dashboard now includes seamless PDF and CSV export options for both the summary and detailed assessment views. Instructors can easily download and share reports with a single click, ensuring a smooth transition between digital and offline workflows.

Dynamic Sorting, Searching, and Filtering:
In the summary view, instructors can effortlessly sort, search, and filter data by IDs and student names, enabling quick access to relevant information and ensuring streamlined navigation through large datasets.

Comprehensive Statistics View:
A statistics module has been integrated to provide visual insights into assessment data. This includes:
Team and student averages displayed in a clean, intuitive format.
Pie charts with assorted colors for clear, visually engaging representations of performance trends and distribution.
